IT Planning & Leadership

With alignment with your organization’s overall strategy, Information Technology needs to plan and execute to enable your organization to meet its strategic goals.

IT Planning & Leadership

JoyeGroup IT Leaders can:

  • Provide an Information Technology (IT) Strategic Plan to meet and support Company objectives
  • Develop an IT department Mission to be an “Enabler” to your business
  • Organize and manage your IT group to be more effective in meeting Company goals
  • Define IT job descriptions and hire needed IT talent to deliver successful projects
  • Establish IT succession plans to ensure your organization is not crippled if someone leaves
  • Provide/Establish a Project Management Organization (PMO) to better communicate and manage your projects
  • Deploy a Project Management Methodology to ensure your projects are consistently executed
  • Establish System Documentation standards to ensure systems and updates are documented to allow future updates more efficient and less costly
  • Create disaster recovery Business Continuity Plans to quickly recovery from an unexpected disaster
  • Develop a solid backup process to restore information quickly if it is lost or corrupted
  • Assess and recommend improvements for your company’s Security Posture

With a proper focus on IT it will be an Enabler for your business and will tightly couple with your company’s strategic plan.
