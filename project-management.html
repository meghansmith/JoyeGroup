IT Leadership and CIO Services

With alignment with your organization’s overall strategy, Information Technology needs to plan and execute to enable your organization to meet its strategic goals.

JoyeGroup IT Leaders can provide/develop/establish:

  • IT organizational chart to clearly define roles and responsibilities to meet company goals
  • IT succession planning to ensure your organization is not crippled if someone leaves IT
  • Project Management Organization (PMO), to better communicate and manage your projects
  • Project Management Methodology to ensure your projects are consistently executed
  • System Documentation standards to ensure systems and updates are documented to allow future updates more efficient and less costly
  • Disaster recovery Business Continuity Plans to quickly recovery from an unexpected disaster
  • Solid backup process to restore information quickly if it is lost or corrupted
