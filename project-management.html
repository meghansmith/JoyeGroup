Project & Schedule Management

A JoyeGroup Project Manager will ensure your project is planned, managed, developed, and delivered to approved requirements.

Project Management Services

Your organization will benefit with the following Project Management actions, deliverables, and knowledge:

  • Clearly documented project requirements to ensure your requirements are pursued and satisfied
  • Provide focus for your project to be completed
  • Clearly communicate the needed talent and skills to complete the project
  • Produce a detailed project schedule to manage time, cost, and scope
  • Provide consistent project communication updates to you and your executive team
  • Expertise in both "Waterfall" and "Agile" SDLC Methodologies
  • Project closure communication to you when requirements are met
  • Produce needed deliverables to demonstrate progress, for example:
    • Status Reporting
    • Project Charter
    • Scope
    • Project Plan
    • Product Design Documents
    • Product or process development
    • Training materials
    • Documentation
    • Test plan and results
    • Implementation Plan

Project Schedule Management and Earned Value

The following Schedule actions will help your organization complete projects:

  • Understand the project and define project deliverables
  • Build a Microsoft Project schedule containing work tasks, resources, costs, work estimates, predecessors, successors, and other task details
  • Update the schedule with work progress and completion
  • If work is not completed as planned, progress the unfinished tasks to later delivery
  • Produce reports and views of planned near term work
  • Produce planned value and earned value data to manage costs
  • Meet with you often, The Customer, to communicate project status
