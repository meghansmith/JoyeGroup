Process

Projects will have better success at your company by proceeding through phases defined and directed by JoyeGroup. This is important because it ensures that:

  • Your requirements (what needs to be done defined by you) are documented and satisfied
  • What is produced is tested to ensure your requirements are satisfied
  • Deliverables are produced for your project as phases are completed
  • If your project phase results are not achieved, as a team we will review the results and determine if the project proceeds. Stopping a project is an option to protect your investment

Principle Guided Partnerships

JoyeGroup is Dedicated to Client Success

Our principles guide our people, our relationships and your work. JoyeGroup strives to uphold a flawless reputation in the marketplace. The principles below are our commitment to your success.

  1. Best Interest – You, our customer, is our focus. JoyeGroup team members will put your best interest first before ours.
  2. Trust – Your trust is mandatory for partnerships to be effective. With trust, JoyeGroup can produce results faster by coming to conclusions more rapidly.
  3. Profitability – Customer positive profitability is necessary for projects. This is a necessary project ingredient and will be reviewed at the project beginning, execution, and closure.
  4. Confidentiality – Your company information is part of your competitive advantage and our team will keep all client information 100% confidential.
  5. High Quality – To achieve your project expected results, deliverables will be measured for quality. If your project is not meeting quality targets, adjustments will be discussed and implemented.
  6. Our Team – To provide high performing JoyeGroup team members for you, JoyeGroup invests in our people’s future both in and out of work through a multitude of channels such as professional development programs as well as specific technical training courses
  7. Innovation – Your organization and projects are unique, JoyeGroup will stress creativity and imagination to solve your problems quickly.
  8. Customer Focus—We understand that your satisfaction will drive our future business
