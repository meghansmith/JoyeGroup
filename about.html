Process

Projects will have better success at your company by proceeding through phases defined and directed by JoyeGroup. This is important because it ensures that:

  • Your requirements (what needs to be done defined by you) are documented and satisfied
  • What is produced is tested to ensure your requirements are satisfied
  • Deliverables are produced for your project as phases are completed
  • If your project phase results are not achieved, as a team we will review the results and determine if the project proceeds. Stopping a project is an option to protect your investment

Founder: Chris Joye

Joye's IT career has spanned more than 38 years, including work as a software developer, designer, project manager, and IT Executive. When he saw a need for experienced IT Consultants, he built JoyeGroup. JoyeGroup has worked in industries from Banking to Manufacturing, from Insurance to Governmental agencies, and has successfully lead organizations to improved IT investments and returns.

As a CIO/IT Executive, Chris Joye has managed, mentored, and led hundreds of people with information engineering solutions. As a CEO, he was Chairman of the Board of Commissioners of the 5th largest SC college, responsible for an $84 million budget. He's become a Cyber Security Expert, leveraging his knowledge to both provide remediation support and to help companies strengthen their security posture before an attack.

Some of Joye's major project successes:

  • As IT manager for the new Ingersoll-Rand (IR) Worldwide Strategic Sourcing Division, he implemented a Data Warehouse that assisted in saving IR $100 million in the first year.
  • In the Project Manager role, he oversaw a successful implementation of SAP (ERP) for the State of SC. This project saved the Government over $25 million annually by consolidating legacy information systems.

Chris Joye is a lifelong learner with a passion for Information Technology. His educational background includes:

  • BS Industrial Management focused on Computer Science
  • MBA with Highest Honors from UNC Greensboro
  • Certifications in Security+, Professional Management of Projects (PMP) and Certified Internet Webmaster (CIW)
